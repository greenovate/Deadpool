STREAK ANNOUNCER (plays on kill streaks):
Drop these files to enable announcer voice lines.
The addon plays them automatically at the right streak count.

  doublekill.mp3     - 2 kills    "DOUBLE KILL"
  triplekill.mp3     - 3 kills    "TRIPLE KILL"
  quadkill.mp3       - 4 kills    "QUAD KILL"
  rampage.mp3        - 5 kills    "RAMPAGE"
  killingspree.mp3   - 6 kills    "KILLING SPREE"
  dominating.mp3     - 7 kills    "DOMINATING"
  unstoppable.mp3    - 8 kills    "UNSTOPPABLE"
  megakill.mp3       - 9 kills    "MEGA KILL"
  godlike.mp3        - 10 kills   "GODLIKE"
  holyshit.mp3       - 15 kills   "HOLY SHIT"
  wickedsick.mp3     - 20 kills   "WICKED SICK"
  monsterkill.mp3    - 25 kills   "MONSTER KILL"
  firstblood.mp3     - (reserved for future use)

WHERE TO GET SOUNDS:
- Search "Unreal Tournament announcer sounds mp3"
- Search "Quake 3 announcer sound pack"
- Any short .mp3/.wav/.ogg clips will work

The addon plays the HIGHEST matching tier.
Example: at 12 kills, it plays "GODLIKE" (tier 10).
At 15 kills, it plays "HOLY SHIT" (tier 15).
